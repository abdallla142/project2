Sole Roll

Dribbling

A simple yet effective move to change direction or evade a defender using the sole of your foot.

Watch & Learn

Watch on YouTube

Step by Step

  1. Place the sole of your foot on top of the ball.
  2. Roll the ball across your body to the other foot, moving it away from a defender.
  3. Use your body to shield the ball as you roll it.
  4. Be ready to dribble away with the other foot immediately after the roll.

Pro Tips

Practice the move quickly to make it effective in a game. Keep your head up to see where defenders are and where you want to go. Combine the sole roll with other dribbling moves for greater deception. Maintain a low center of gravity.
