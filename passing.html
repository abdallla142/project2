Passing

Fundamental

Master accurate short and long passes to teammates. Learn proper technique for power and precision.

Watch & Learn

Watch on YouTube

Step by Step

  1. Position your non-kicking foot next to the ball, pointing toward your target.
  2. Keep your eyes on the ball and your head steady as you approach.
  3. Use the inside of your foot for accuracy on short passes.
  4. Follow through with your kicking foot toward your target for better control.

Pro Tips

Practice passing with both feet to become more versatile. For long passes, lean back slightly and use the laces of your foot. Always pass to where your teammate is moving, not where they are. Weight your pass correctly: too soft and it's intercepted, too hard and it's difficult to control.
