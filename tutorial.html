Step-by-Step Tutorials

Detailed breakdowns to help you master each skill. Each tutorial includes step-by-step instructions, tips, and practice guidance.

Dribbling Basics

Step 1: Keep the ball close to your feet using the inside and outside of your foot
Step 2: Maintain a low center of gravity for better balance
Step 3: Use your body to shield the ball from defenders
Step 4: Practice changing direction quickly with small touches

Pro Tip: Keep your head up to see the field while dribbling

Passing Techniques

Step 1: Plant your non-kicking foot next to the ball
Step 2: Lock your ankle and strike through the center of the ball
Step 3: Follow through in the direction you want the ball to go
Step 4: Use the inside of your foot for accuracy, laces for power

Pro Tip: Weight your passes correctly: not too hard, not too soft

Shooting Fundamentals

Step 1: Position yourself at the correct angle to the goal
Step 2: Approach the ball with confidence and purpose
Step 3: Strike with your laces for power or inside foot for placement
Step 4: Keep your body over the ball to keep shots low and accurate

Pro Tip: Practice both placement and power shots for versatility

Juggling Mastery

Step 1: Start with the ball in your hands and drop it to your foot
Step 2: Use the top of your foot (laces) with a slight upward motion
Step 3: Keep your foot relaxed and cushion the ball on contact
Step 4: Progress from one touch to alternating feet

Pro Tip: Start with thigh juggles before moving to feet for better control
